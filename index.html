Solving Civic issues using Technology

Coimbatore is in need of smart individuals working towards building a smart city. Time for kids to solve the civic issues in coimbatore using Technology. We're inviting top 25 school teams to join us for a 1 week Coding Camp to create solutions for social problems. Kids will learn to build an Android App using MIT App Inventor 2 and work towards building a technology solutions to help build a Smart Kovai.

TECH JAMBOREE 2016 is hosted by

November 7-13, 2016

FAQ

Application problems?
If you need to update your Problem or solution (to add on team members or any other reason) then please resubmit your app and we will consider your latest submission.
What if I don't have a team or an idea?
Your team mate need not be from the same class. Just go reach out to your school principal or computer staff to help you find the right team mate. Make sure you find out a team mate who has complemetary skills as of yours. Else try building one yourself. There are many kids hacking all alone.
Who can attend?
All school going kids of 11-15 years of age.
How much does it cost?
Cal Hacks is free for all accepted hackers. We will provide your WiFi, meals, caffeine, swag, and workspace for the entire weekend.
How big should teams be? Can I hack alone?
We highly encourage you to build with a team. Teamwork is deeply rooted in the hacking ethos, and you'll have a better time building with your brightest peers. Teams should be at most two people.
Why Tech Jamboree? Can we kids Code?
Tech Jamboree is an intiative to get kids into serious coding. Computer Science is not about just using Computers. Across the globe Computer coding has been inculcated in primary schools as the Computer coding has become like the next English Skill. Also kids (you guys) are really smart than what we adults were at your age. We are sure, you would go far beyound expectation.
What can I build?
You would building an Android Mobile app using MIT App Inventor 2. Any type of technology solution that can solve the toughest civic challenges you come across living in Coimbatore is most welcome. Projects will be judged based on creativity, right problem, relevant solution and utility by a panel of industry judges.
What should I bring?
All attendees are required to bring their valid student ID for admission. Laptops (if any), head phones, Lunch, Snack and water bottle are highly recommended.
What if I have another question?
Please feel free to reach out to us via Facebook or email at support@themango.co.
